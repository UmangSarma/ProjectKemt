About KEMT

Miss. Evangeline Dhinakaran, fondly known as 'Angel' was the only daughter of our revered Founder and Chancellor Emeritus (Late) Dr. D.G.S. Dhinakaran. She had a great desire to raise young people of her age to the highest echelons of the society to glorify God. She left for her eternal abode on 21st May,1986, a few months before Karunya University was established.

Instructions

Events Basket ball (Men and Women), Volleyball (Men and Women), Kabaddi (Men and Women), Football (Men) and Hockey (Men) matches are the major events conducted at South India Level from 21st to 24th September 2016. These matches are open to all Colleges and Universities located across South India. The teams are to be strictly comprised of bonafide students only. Updated Rules and Regulations framed by the Inter-University Sports Board of India (AIU) will be followed. Students pursuing courses through Open University and correspondent or distance mode are not eligible to participate in the Tournament. However, student sportspersons of other nationalities are permitted as per AIU rules. ID card issued by the competent authorities of the respective Institutions must be produced on demand by the Organizing Committee / Umpires / Referees for verification. Transportation from Gandhipuram Bus Station, Coimbatore to Karunya University will be arranged on request with prior intimation.
Kindly contact Transportation Committee (contact number)-94878 46514
